Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

1. Our Philosophy: On-Device Privacy

AdvisorNote is built on the principle of absolute privacy. Our core promise is that your client data and transcripts **never leave your device**. We do not use cloud-based transcription, telemetry, or data collection.

  • **Zero Audio Data:** We do not capture, store, or transmit your audio or transcriptions to any external server.
  • **Local Processing:** All speech-to-text processing is performed locally on your computer using our custom-trained models.
  • **No Telemetry:** We do not track application usage, feature usage, or user activity within the application.

2. Information We Collect (Only for Billing & Support)

We only collect the minimum amount of information necessary for account management and providing technical support.

  • **Contact Information:** Name and email address (provided during trial sign-up or purchase).
  • **Billing Information:** If you purchase a license, payment details are processed by our secure third-party payment provider (e.g., Stripe) and are never stored on our servers.
  • **License Information:** We store a cryptographically-signed license key to verify your subscription status. This key is non-identifying.

3. Data Security and Storage

Since all sensitive data is processed and stored locally, the security of that data relies on your device's security protocols.

  • **Local File Storage:** Any transcription files you choose to save are stored exclusively on your device (e.g., your local documents folder).
  • **Data Location:** We guarantee that no Australian client data or audio is stored, processed, or transferred outside of Australia by AdvisorNote.
  • **Website Cookies:** Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for website functionality (e.g., navigation, language settings). We do not use invasive tracking cookies.

4. Your Rights

As an Australian user, you have rights regarding your personal information under the Australian Privacy Act.

  • **Access:** You can request a copy of any personal information we hold about you
  • **Opt out:** Opt out of marketing communications

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@advisornote.com
